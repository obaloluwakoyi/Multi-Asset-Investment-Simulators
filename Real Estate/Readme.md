FinSim Analytics Suite: Multi-Asset Investment Simulators
The FinSim Analytics Suite is a collection of high-performance, client-side financial modeling dashboards designed for real-time investment appraisal. Built using a modern stack of HTML5, vanilla JavaScript, and Chart.js, these tools enable investors to simulate complex financial trajectories across residential real estate and hospitality ventures.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript

Data Visualization: Chart.js

Document Generation: html2pdf.js

📊 Core Simulators
1. Realty Analytics Pro (real_estate_proforma.html)
An investment-grade property evaluator designed for long-term acquisitions.

KPI Tracking: Real-time calculation of Cap Rate, Monthly Cashflow, and Cash-on-Cash return.

Equity Projection: Visualizes 10-year equity growth against remaining debt.

Pro-Forma Analysis: Generates a year-over-year breakdown of gross income, operating expenses, and net cashflow.

Reporting: Exports professional "Real Estate Investment Memos" in PDF format.

2. Short-Let ROI Terminal (short_let_simulator.html)
A specialized hospitality yield simulator tailored for short-term rental economics.

Hospitality Logic: Models profitability based on nightly rates, occupancy percentages, and maintenance costs.

Operational Metrics: Automatically determines monthly net operating income (NOI) and break-even nights.

Growth Forecasting: Projects cumulative wealth by combining property appreciation with annual rental revenue growth.

Reporting: Generates "Executive Investment Memos" optimized for hospitality asset stakeholders.
